## Algebra Foundations

### Exponents

<div class="math">
<p>
 \( 8^{3} \),  \( 3^{4} \),  \( 4^{8} \)
</p>
 <p>
 \( \frac{1}{5}^{3} \) = \( \frac{1}{5} \times \frac{1}{5} \times \frac{1}{5} \) = \( \frac{1}{125} \)
</p>
</div>

### Exponent Properties
1. Multiply Powers

  <div class="math">
  <p>
  (\( 8^{3})^{4} \) = \( 8^{12} \)
  </p>
  <p>
  \( (5^{3})^{2} \) = \( 5^{6} \)
  </p>
  </div>
  
2. Divide Powers

  <div class="math">
  <p>
  \(\frac{4^6}{4^x}\)
  </p>
  <p>
  \(\frac{a^6}{a}\)
  </p>
  </div>
  
3. Powers of Products and Quotients

  <div class="math">
  <p>
  \( (3 \times 6)^3 \) = \( 3^{3} \times 6^{3} \) = \( 3^{3} \times 6^{3} \)
  </p>
  <p>
  (\(8^{3})^{4}\) = \( 8^{12} \)
  </p>
  </div>
