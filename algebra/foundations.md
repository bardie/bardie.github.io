### Exponent Properties
1. Multiply Powers
  <div class="math">
  <p>
  (\( 8^{3} \))^{4} = \( 8^{12} \)
  </p>
  <p>
  (\( 5^{3} \))^{2} = \( 5^{6} \)
  </p>
  </div>
2. Divide Powers
  <div class="math">
  <p>
  \(\frac{4^6}{4^x}}\)
  </p>
  <p>
  \(\frac{a^6}{a}\)
  </p>
  </div>
